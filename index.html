Visa & Residency Programs

Panama offers some of the most attractive residency programs in the world. Find the right path for you.

FOR RETIREES

Pensionado Visa

REQUIREMENTS

  • Minimum pension of $1,000/month from a government or private source
  • Proof of pension income (certified letter)
  • Valid passport with 6+ months validity
  • Police clearance from country of origin
  • Health certificate

KEY BENEFITS

  • 50% off entertainment & hotels
  • 25% off airline tickets
  • 25% off restaurants
  • 15% off hospital bills
  • Tax exemption on household goods import

FOR CITIZENS OF 50+ COUNTRIES

Friendly Nations Visa

REQUIREMENTS

  • Citizen of one of the 50 approved 'Friendly Nations'
  • Establish economic ties (employment, business, or bank deposit of $5,000+)
  • Valid passport and police clearance
  • Proof of professional or economic activity in Panama
  • No minimum income requirement

KEY BENEFITS

  • Path to permanent residency
  • Work permit included
  • Can open a business in Panama
  • Eligible for citizenship after 5 years
  • Includes dependents (spouse & children)

FOR INVESTORS

Qualified Investor Visa

REQUIREMENTS

  • Invest minimum $300,000 in Panama real estate
  • Or invest $500,000 in a Panamanian business or fixed-term deposit
  • Reforestation investment of $100,000+ also qualifies
  • Proof of legal source of investment funds
  • Clean criminal record

KEY BENEFITS

  • Immediate permanent residency
  • No need to establish economic ties
  • Tax benefits on investments
  • Can apply for citizenship after 5 years
  • Family members can be included

FOR INDEPENDENT INCOME EARNERS

Self-Economic Solvency Visa

REQUIREMENTS

  • Fixed-term deposit of $300,000 in a Panamanian bank
  • Or purchase real estate worth $300,000+
  • Additional $2,000 per dependent
  • Police clearance and health certificate
  • Proof of legal source of funds

KEY BENEFITS

  • Permanent residency from day one
  • No employment or business requirement
  • Full access to Panama's banking system
  • Eligible for citizenship after 5 years
  • Import household goods duty-free

LEGAL FRAMEWORK

What You Need to Know

Panama's legal framework is designed to attract foreign investment and retirees. Here are the key legal advantages of making Panama your new home.

Territorial Tax System

Panama only taxes income generated within its borders. Foreign-sourced income — including pensions, dividends, and remote work earnings — is completely tax-free.

US Dollar Economy

The Panamanian Balboa is pegged 1:1 to the US Dollar, and USD is legal tender. No currency exchange risk for American retirees and investors.

Property Ownership Rights

Foreigners can own property in Panama with the same rights as citizens. Title registration is straightforward through the Public Registry.

Banking Privacy

Panama maintains strong banking secrecy laws. Opening a bank account as a foreign resident is accessible with proper documentation.
